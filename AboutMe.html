Early Life

Even as a young kid I always had a deep love for computers and the hardware associated with them. While the other kids was playing football. I was building my own PC's at just 12 years old with old hardware that I would find in junkyards, or in a storage building that I was helping clean just to try and get something working. I'm talking the old stuff, back when motherboards still had ide, AGP, and no pci express slots and pre ddr; the ram used was sdram. Where we had megabytes not gigabytes to work with. I still remember thinking how awesome it was when I found my first stick of 512mb sdram; I was so excited when it was compatible and the pc posted and booted up. Heck I even had a couple of motherboards that pre-dated pci slots and agp all together. Granted, I never got one of them to boot, but it wasn't for lack of effort.

My current goals and ambitions

My current short term goal is to complete my associates degree. I want to at some point receive my bachelors in Computer science.
