Awesome Game Reviews RoadMap

Author and Designer: Phillip Wood


  1. Home Page: This page will consist of a search bar to search for games of interest, also there will be a section for top rated games of the day. If a top rated game img is clicked then the browser will be directed to the Game details webpage displaying the details of the clicked game. I want to include links to the websites of my favorite games on the side bar.


  2. Advanced Search Page: This page will have different filters that can be applied to the search, for instance someone might only want to view FPS games. There will also be a link back to the home page from here.


  3. Game Details Page: This page will display the information of the game: name, summary, screenshots, short video clips, and reviews of people that played the game. There will also be a link back to the home page from this page or advanced search page.


  4. About Me Page: This page will give a brief description of me, my goals and the love of video games. I will include a few screenshot of my in game characters in my favorite games and maybe how to contact me on those characters if the game is multiplayer.


  5. Contact Me Page: I will include links to my social media and create a html form with subject and description boxes that emails me when submitted.
